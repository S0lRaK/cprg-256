Executive Summary

This report will help the audience understand the pros and cons of the 3D printing technology, and show some of the possibilities as medical applications. The scope of it includes general aspects of the technology and its applications in hospitals. The method used to obtain the information is online research. This reports states some trends of 3D Printing, its advantages and disadvantages, its medical applications and hospital, and the revenue surveyed and projected. Based on this facts, the benefits are higher for any hospital that will use 3D Printing in their environment.
